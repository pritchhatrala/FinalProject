📚 About NotesHub

Welcome to NotesHub — a platform created for students, by students.

🎯 Our Mission

NotesHub is built to help students share quality academic notes, assignments, and study materials with each other easily and efficiently. We aim to promote collaborative learning.

⚙️ How It Works

  • Students upload their notes through the platform.
  • Admin reviews and either approves or rejects the submissions.
  • Approved notes become available for download/viewing by others.

👩‍🎓 Who Can Use It?

- College Students
- School Students
- Teachers looking for shared resources
